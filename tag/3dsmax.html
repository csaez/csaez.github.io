Speaking Maxscript

  • October 31, 2013
  • Cesar Saez
  • Comments

Hi guys,

Today a friend asked for a quick way to create independent copies/instances of an object at each vertex position of another mesh in 3dsmax.

I haven't worked with 3dsmax for a long time, but it seemed to be a job for maxscript and she knows nothing about scripting, so I took a deep breath and gave it a shot.

for v in $[1].mesh.verts do (instance $[2]).pos = v.pos * $[1].transform

Here's the thing: maxscript turned out to be surprisingly straight forward, I mean, this kind of stuff could be done in any DCC, but things like operator overloading or grouping by parentheses really help to lower the barrier and keep things simple ...
