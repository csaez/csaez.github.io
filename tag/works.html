about me

Hi there, my name is César and I love figure out how things work... yep, I was one of those kids who question everything. That passion led me to get a BESc, but at some point in my career I met computer graphics and I fell in love.

Since then, I have had the opportunity to experience different cultures and meet amazing people while traveling around the world working as character technical director.

If you have any comments, questions or some snide remarks; just drop me a line.
